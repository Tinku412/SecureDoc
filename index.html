How it works

Four steps from upload to insight.

1

Upload your PDF

Sign in with Google and drop your file. It is stored privately — nobody can reach it without going through you.

2

Lock it to an email

Enter the recipient's email address. A unique link is generated that only works for that person.

3

They verify, then read

Your recipient enters their email and a one-time code. The document opens with their identity watermarked on every page.

4

You see everything

Your dashboard shows every open: who, when, from which IP, total reading time, and time spent per page.
